Why Hack Start?

At Hack Start, we promise to curate the best content possible and try to provide mentorship for team building, ideation, basic skills, and technical workshops to encourage those with little or no experienceto try out new opportunities like hackathons, coding and anything possible in the Coding Verse!
If you believe you are someone who is passionate about learning and want to create a project from start to finish, Hack Start is the place for you.

FAQ

What is Hack Start?

Hack Start is our initiative to introduce the hackathon space and new edge tech to beginners from all backgrounds interested in technology.

What if I don't have any coding or hacking experience?

It's absolutely fine if you don't! This program is designed for beginners, and you don't need prior coding or hackathon experience at all.

Who can apply to Hack Start?

Unfortunately, this program is only open for students enrolled at VIT Chennai, but students of ALL grades and majors are encouraged to apply!

If I get accepted to Hack Club, do I get accepted to Hack Start?

Yes! Hack Club members will be able to participate in Hack Start.
